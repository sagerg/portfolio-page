Hi! I'm Sage Garcia. I code & design things.|

Currently, I'm a Computer Science major at the University of Maryland Baltimore County. I started my journey in software development while I was in Manila, participating in organizations such as AIESEC, YOUTH HACK, Management Information Systems Association, and UX Philippines.

Something I enjoyed more than coding was collaborating with people as well, whether they were students, mentors, or professionals. This lead me to join User Experience Society as a Community Manager. This allowed me to build partnerships with other organizations and to meet new people alike.

I also enjoyed educating people who were eager to learn more about software development. So I decided to join Coder Kids over at McLean, VA for the Fall of 2019. For the Spring of 2020, I became a Teaching Assistant at my local community college, tutoring students about languages such as C++, Java, and Python.
